FAQ

Questions You're Probably Already Asking

What kind of businesses do you work with?
We build for businesses where the website has a real job to do — service businesses, trades, clinics, shops and e-commerce, restaurants, and growing brands. If your site should be bringing in work and isn't, that's exactly what we fix.
How much does a project cost?
Every project is custom-quoted to scope, and our pricing stays very competitive for the quality you get. Once we understand what you need, you'll get a clear, fixed price before any work begins — no surprises later.
How long does a build take?
Most sites launch in about two weeks from kickoff. Simple one-pagers can be faster; larger builds with custom features take a little longer.
What happens after launch?
We don't vanish once the site is live. You get two weeks of close attention right after launch — we keep an eye on how it's running, fix anything that comes up, and make small tweaks. After that, ongoing support is included, so there's always someone to call.
Can I edit the site myself afterwards?
For most of our clients, we handle the edits for you — just send over what you'd like changed and we take care of it. Prefer to manage it yourself? We can build the site so you can edit it directly; whether that's the right fit depends on the design we choose together.
What makes Parallex different?
Most web studios are run by designers, so they optimize for how a site looks. We start from the job your site has to do — turn visitors into customers — and design backward from there. We also make it painless: we handle the heavy lifting, move fast, and keep you out of the technical weeds. And if you don't have your wording or photos ready, we'll polish what you give us — or create it for you.
