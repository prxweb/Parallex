FAQ

Questions You're Probably Already Thinking About

What industries do you work with?
We work with businesses where the website actually has a job to do — e-commerce, service businesses, clinics, construction, SaaS, jewellery, hospitality. If yours should be bringing in revenue and isn't, we can help.
Why not just update my existing site?
Most sites we see have structural problems, not cosmetic ones: slow code, weak positioning, and no clear path to conversion. Patching the surface rarely fixes the deeper issues. A rebuild lets us address the root cause — structure, copy, layout, and conversion mechanics.
How much does a project cost?
Projects typically range from $5K to $100K+ depending on scope and complexity. We'll share a custom quote once we understand exactly what you need.
How long does a rebuild take?
Most projects launch in 6–9 weeks from kickoff. Smaller scopes wrap in 4–5 weeks. Full e-commerce rebuilds with custom integrations can run 10–12.
How do I know this will get me more leads?
Two parts. A strategy phase maps your buyer journey with conversion mechanics built in, and we install analytics with baseline KPI tracking so the results are measurable — not guessed at.
What happens after launch?
Three things. Every project includes a 30-day post-launch window where we monitor performance, fix bugs, and make small adjustments. We hand over with documentation and a training session. And if you want ongoing optimisation, we offer monthly support.
Can I edit the site myself afterwards?
Yes. We build on platforms your team can actually manage — Webflow, WordPress, Shopify — depending on what fits your business. You get documentation and a training session at handover.
What makes Parallex different?
Most web design agencies are run by designers. We're a performance marketing team that builds websites. We run paid traffic for clients every day across 40+ businesses, so we see in real time what converts visitors and what makes them bounce.
